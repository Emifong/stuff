Project 1: Oblique Strategies

Group Name: Group Art 103 from Art 103

Members: Vana, Austin, Guadalupe, Charli, Emily F.(ME)


"Change instrument roles"


For oblique strategies, we generated three strategies and had to choose one. So our three choices were:

1) Not building a wall but making a brick

2) Change intrument roles

3) What mistakes did you make last time?


Of course, from the heading above, we chose number 2. Number 1 didn't ring any ideas for us and number 3 involved us being vulnerable. So number 2 was just right for us. We brainstormed ideas about tools and objects that switch roles or change their intended purpose. From the brainstorm we came up with the concepts below.


Sushi Roles-This trumpet has been given a new role inside of a sushi roll.


Soup Spoon-The bowl and spoon are made of macaroni and soup and inside the bowl is filled with melted metal.


Air Horn-Instead of someone blowing into the horn, the breath is replaced by air from a bike pump. An instrument used by another instrument.


Palette-The palette is the plate and paint brush is the spoon. The crayon tips are gummies and charcoal sticks are dark chocolate. The other circular palette, spray can, and acrylic paint tube are cakes.


Door-The door is a electronic device that connect to this room, when we pull the door handle, it is no longer make the door open. It gives the room a new environment.
